# Nexus Platform — Demo Script

A suggested walkthrough for the final demo video/presentation (Milestone 7). Aim for 5–8 minutes.
Use the two demo accounts on the login screen ("Entrepreneur Demo" / "Investor Demo" buttons)
to switch perspectives quickly.

## 1. Intro (30s)
- One sentence on what Nexus is: a platform connecting investors and entrepreneurs.
- Mention this demo covers everything added across the 3-week internship: scheduling, video
  calls, document e-signing, payments, and security.

## 2. Login & Security (1 min)
- Show the role picker (Entrepreneur / Investor) on the login screen.
- Log in with the Entrepreneur demo account → show the **2FA step**: 6-digit OTP boxes,
  mention the demo code shown on screen, submit it.
- Briefly show the **password strength meter** on the Register page (type a weak → strong
  password to show the bar change color).

## 3. Dashboard & Guided Tour (1 min)
- Land on the Entrepreneur dashboard, point out the stat cards (Pending Requests, Connections,
  Upcoming Meetings, Wallet Balance).
- Click **"Take a tour"** in the navbar to show the guided walkthrough highlighting Dashboard →
  Calendar → Document Chamber → Wallet.

## 4. Scheduling Calendar (1 min)
- Go to **Calendar**. Add an availability slot for tomorrow.
- Show an incoming meeting request, accept it, and point out it now appears as a confirmed
  meeting on the dashboard.

## 5. Messaging & Video Call (1 min)
- Open **Messages**, pick a conversation.
- Click the video icon to launch the **Video Call** mock: show local camera preview, mute/unmute,
  camera toggle, and (if convenient) a quick screen-share toggle. End the call.

## 6. Document Chamber (1–1.5 min)
- Go to **Document Chamber**. Upload a sample PDF, assign it to a counterpart.
- Move it from Draft → In Review → Sign it with the signature pad.
- Show the status badge updating to "Signed" and open the preview modal to show the signature.

## 7. Wallet / Payments (1 min)
- Switch to the **Investor** demo account (log out, log back in, or just narrate "as an investor…").
- Go to **Wallet**, show the balance card.
- Do a **"Fund a Deal"** transfer to an entrepreneur, then show it land in the transaction
  history table with a "Deal Funding" type tag.

## 8. Wrap-up (30s)
- Recap the roles: Investor vs Entrepreneur dashboards/navigation differ.
- Mention this is a frontend-only prototype — no real backend, payments, or video signaling
  server — everything is simulated with in-memory mock data, per the assignment scope.

---

### Recording tips
- Use your OS screen recorder (Windows: Xbox Game Bar / Snipping Tool; Mac: QuickTime;
  or OBS Studio on either) — this needs to happen on your machine, not in this environment.
- Keep the browser window at a normal desktop width so the responsive layouts look their best.
- If camera/mic permission prompts appear during the Video Call section, accept them ahead of
  time so the recording isn't interrupted.
